About Us

We specialize in **train & rail asset painting**—from full body refinishes to touch-ups and compliance markings. Our teams follow documented safety procedures and deliver durable, weather-resistant finishes that protect metal and keep fleets looking sharp.

Rail Focus: Locomotives, coaches, tankers, cargo cars, and MOW equipment.
Durable Systems: Epoxy primers, polyurethanes, anti-graffiti clear coats.
Mobile Crews: On-site containment, surface prep, and application.
Quality Docs: Color codes, coating specs, and inspection reports.

Our Formula (10 Key Points)

  1. Site Safety: Permits, lock-out/tag-out, PPE, and ventilation plans.
  2. Surface Prep: Degrease, blast/abrade, profile checks, and cleanliness grades.
  3. Spec-Driven: Coating systems matched to climate, service, and substrate.
  4. Environmental Care: Containment, overspray control, and waste handling.
  5. Application Quality: WFT/DFT gauges, cure windows, and adhesion tests.
  6. Brand Accuracy: Color matching (RAL/Pantone), stencils, and decals.
  7. Anti-Graffiti: Clear coats that make cleaning fast and safe.
  8. Documentation: Daily logs, photos, and final inspection reports.
  9. Scheduling: Work windows coordinated with operations to limit downtime.
  10. Aftercare: Touch-up kits, cleaning guides, and warranty support.
